随笔 - Laplace算子的旋转不变性

发表于 2021-09-23 更新于 2021-11-12 分类于 随笔 ， 数学

一道练习题

定理 (Laplace算子的旋转不变性)

令

  • O=(oij)n×n∈Rn×nO=(o_{ij})_{n\times n}\in\R^{n\times n}O=(oij​)n×n​∈Rn×n 是正交矩阵
  • x=(x1,x2,...,xn)T∈Rnx=(\def\enum#1{x_{ #1}}\enum{1},\enum{2},...,\enum{n})^T\in\R^nx=(x1​,x2​,...,xn​)T∈Rn
  • y=Ox=(y1,y2,...,yn)T∈Rny=Ox=(\def\enum#1{y_{ #1}}\enum{1},\enum{2},...,\enum{n})^T\in\R^ny=Ox=(y1​,y2​,...,yn​)T∈Rn

则

Δx=Δy\Delta_x=\Delta_yΔx​=Δy​

其中

  • Δx:=∑i=1n∂2∂xi2\Delta_x:=\sum_{i=1}^n{\partial^2\over\partial x_i^2}Δx​:=i=1∑n​∂xi2​∂2​

  • Δy:=∑i=1n∂2∂yi2\Delta_y:=\sum_{i=1}^n{\partial^2\over\partial y_i^2}Δy​:=i=1∑n​∂yi2​∂2​

Proof

显然

yi=∑j=1noijxj, ∀i=1..ny_i=\sum_{j=1}^no_{ij}x_j,\ \forall i=1..nyi​=j=1∑n​oij​xj​, ∀i=1..n

故我们有

∂∂xi=∑j=1n∂∂yj∂yj∂xi=∑j=1noji∂∂yj{\partial\over\partial x_i}=\sum_{j=1}^n{\partial\over\partial y_j}{\partial y_j\over\partial x_i}=\sum_{j=1}^no_{ji}{\partial\over\partial y_j}∂xi​∂​=j=1∑n​∂yj​∂​∂xi​∂yj​​=j=1∑n​oji​∂yj​∂​

考虑 Hessian 矩阵

∇x2=(∂2∂xj∂xi)n×n=(∑l=1n∑k=1noljoki∂2∂yl∂yk)n×n\nabla^2_x=\left({\partial^2\over\partial x_j\partial x_i}\right)_{n\times n}=\left(\sum_{l=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^no_{lj}o_{ki}{\partial^2\over\partial y_l\partial y_k}\right)_{n\times n}∇x2​=(∂xj​∂xi​∂2​)n×n​=(l=1∑n​k=1∑n​olj​oki​∂yl​∂yk​∂2​)n×n​

则

Δx=tr⁡(∇x2)=∑l=1n∑k=1n(∑i=1nolioki)∂2∂yl∂yk=∑l=1n∑k=1nδkl∂2∂yl∂yk=∑k=1n∂2∂yk2=Δy\begin{aligned} \Delta_x&=\operatorname{tr}(\nabla^2_x)\\ &=\sum_{l=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n\left(\sum_{i=1}^no_{li}o_{ki}\right){\partial^2\over\partial y_l\partial y_k}\\ &=\sum_{l=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n\delta_{kl}{\partial^2\over\partial y_l\partial y_k}\\ &=\sum_{k=1}^n{\partial^2\over\partial y_k^2}\\ &=\Delta_y\end{aligned}Δx​​=tr(∇x2​)=l=1∑n​k=1∑n​(i=1∑n​oli​oki​)∂yl​∂yk​∂2​=l=1∑n​k=1∑n​δkl​∂yl​∂yk​∂2​=k=1∑n​∂yk2​∂2​=Δy​​
